<p>The Course Study Council (CCS) is the body of the Faculty responsible for the educational aspects of the courses offered. It defines the educational and training objectives in line with the indications and directives of the Faculty to which it belongs. Its tasks include:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Proposing the Course Study Educational Regulations to the School</li>
    <li>Approving the course syllabi</li>
    <li>Handling all matters related to student careers</li>
    <li>Deliberating on matters within its competence</li>
    <li>Course Study Councils</li>
</ul>
<p>Each Course Study Council (CCS) brings together the professors responsible for the courses or teaching modules offered in the Course of Study and a number of elected student representatives.</p>
<p>The composition of the CCS is updated annually by the Dean, in relation to the activation or deactivation of courses as stipulated in the specific Course Study Educational Regulations.</p>
